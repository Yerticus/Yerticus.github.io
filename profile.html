My Bio

Being from Detroit, MI, I have always been a proponent of hard work and dedication. I am always seeking to develop myself through various avenues of work, hobbies and education systems.

I started my plunge into the workforce by becoming a delivery driver at Jimmy John's in Clinton Township, MI. At the same time I was attending Macomb Community College
to complete general education courses. I was primarily enrolled in their Mechatronics program, obtaining basic knowledge in robotics, pneumatic systems,
CNC programming/operation, electrical and mechanical engineering.> I graduated in 2014 with my Associate's Degree in General Studies.

After graduating I wanted to see what career options there were in Michigan. The economy was on a steady decline and most jobs/careers involved the automotive industry.
The Flint Water Crisis didn't help matters either. I started my first semester at Wayne State University in 2015, studying Industrial/Product Design. I decided to take a break
after the first year because I knew there were more fitting opportunities, just not in the comfort of my own backyard.

I moved to North Dallas, TX in 2016 and throughout that first year. I explored Texas, built my social network and I worked at The Colony Home Depot.
I continue my martial arts and self defense training at Plano Martial Arts. By the Summer of 2017, I became a certified kickboxing coach at TITLE Boxing Club in Frisco.
My main goal was to help others achieve their fitness goals and teach the importance of self defense and awareness. From an enthusiast perspective, I spend a lot of time
developing my technique through visiting gyms in the DFW area. I've trained with various teachers, students, pro and amatuer fighters in Boxing, Muay Thai, Taekwondo,
Hapkido, Wing Chun, Kendo/Kenjustu(Japanese Sword) and Traditional Japanese Jujutsu.
